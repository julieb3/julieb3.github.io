Branding and Web Design

Friedt Finishing is a cabinetry and millwork compan located in Saskatoon. After some change in business they we're looking for a new yet familliar brand identity. With a new logo the company felt it was time for a new website which will be released sometime in early 2021.

Cover Design

Sina is a small musician from Toronto who I worked with to create a cover for his latest single "Blue Faces". After disscusion with the artist I was able to create a visual representation of his atmospheric and wavy music through a cover and looping video for spotify.

Small Business

During the the summer of 2020 I started my own business where I marketed and sold bucket hats handmade by myself using second hand materials.

3D Shadowbox

This 6 layer paper shadow box was a project I designed, cut, and assembled. It is an abstract cmposition inspired by my love of canoeing and Ray and Charles Eames' Film, Powers of Ten.

3D Design

In March of 2021 I was assigned a project which originally was meant to create a complex wearable paper object. Due to the lockdown I was able to design and build these shoes from found objects in my apartment.

Information Design

Inspired by Theo Deutinger’s ground breaking work in his book The Ultimate Atlas, I was inspired to use the space I had for this 11x 17 infographic print. I created a graph which compared the landspace of different post secondary campus' in Toronto and the percentage of greenspace within them. I wanted the graph to use up the space on the page so it could be visualized easier. I felt the calming purple and logical green would bring forth a theme of relaxation since being surrounded by nature is been proven to decrease negative thoughts and stress.

Typography and Programming

To create an expressive typographic poster I created a coded program in processing to create the structure and form for a print poster. Like always, I got carried away with coding and ended up with a colorful program with type in motion that was choreographed to music.

Typeface Design

This is font is mashup of the typefaces Playbill, Texas Tango, and Monument Extended Bold. The new and bizarre urban country style of the song Old Town Road inspired me to find an old western font in the school library and combine it with a fresh typeface from pangram pangram foundry.

Animation

This was the original pitch for a logo identity for a motavational speaker. The client was drawn to bold type with distortion and the since the name was "Don't Settle" I pitched that the distortion could relate to unsettled water. After some expirementation I created this animation by printing the normal logo and weighing down the paper in the bottom of a bath tub. I then (carefully) set up lights and shot a video on my phone parralell to the waters surface and recorded the water being disrupted by my hand. I then digitized it and used the frames to creat this looping animation.

Booklet

This book was all about the letter Y! This book starts by looking at the history and form of the letter Y. It continues on to discuss the history of the YMCA along with an imagined advocate campaign this company could have. It has a bold colours which include the red used by the YMCA and was printed on opal paper which was warm and bright much like the YMCA is.

Educational Visualization

This four image piece was created for the University of Saskatchewan's Sociology dept. It was made to represent a concept relating to the four stages of learning. This was the first thing I ever made on illustrator and like all new things I learn, I do my reserch and take my time to make something strong.

Visual Art

The 12 images in this portfolio are pastel drawings of my high school peers in a colection titles 'Innate Youth'. I created these pieces to express the emotional, naive, and natural states teens go through as they learn to become adults. It was my detailed work, strong theme, and impressionist inspiration that awarded me a score of 5 in the Advanced Placement Art Program.
