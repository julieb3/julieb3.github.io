    Two Truths and a Dream

    Film Production and Set Design

    Released Summer 2022

    ❮ ❯

    Two Truths and a Dream is a story of a young man who deals with the loss of his abusive father who's memory haunts him. This project was a large learning curve as I was responsible for the design, planning, material selection, and build of a large fake german expressionist hallway. Inspired by The Cabinet of Dr Caligari and Coraline, this hallway was supposed to look otherworldly, distorted, and endless. Additionally I did all of the prop shopping, set dressing, costume selection, and prop design for this short film thesis project. I also created a custom sign to cover up the real one as shown in the slideshow above.
