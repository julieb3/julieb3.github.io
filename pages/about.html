About Me!

Hi I'm Juliette. After growing up in Saskatoon and getting my Honours Bachelor of Design in Toronto, I decided to venture off to the Netherlands to improve my Dutch and learn more about the country's amazing design culture. After a month of travelling on my own I have decided to settle in the Netherlands for the next year by getting my working holiday visa as of September 2022.

I have a strong thirst for knowledge which has led me to learn about many different streams of design. My favourite kinds of jobs involve difficult problem solving, hands on work, design research and improving the world we live in. That is why I consider myself to be best described as an information and experiential designer. My goal is to explore the country and visit as many design exhibits as I can and hopefully get the chance to work with a studio in Noord Brabant or Zoud Holland.

When I'm not working on or reading about design, I'm usually reading about green energy, playing with code, doing yoga, or out rock climbing. As a huge music lover, I enjoy listening to records and playing my guitar. I love learning about philosophy and spending time at the library exploring books and like to stay active by going to the gym. I think my love for many things is what has led me to be a designer since design is something that touches every facet of our lives.
