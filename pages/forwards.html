    Forwards

    Film Production Design

    Released Summer 2022

    ❮ ❯

    Forwards is a short film about a divorced father who struggles to connect with his son and manage his alcoholism. For this piece I was responsible for pre production planning, prop shopping, set dressing, costume, and special effects. I also designed and applied a set of mock alcohol labels inspired by mexican culture, a custom flask cover, and the casting posters for the film. This project was definetly a challenge for me with set backs such as not having access to my usual printing facilities. I was also studying full time, working on another film, and planning a large scale event at the time of this project, which made it crucial that I manage my time to meet all of my deadlines.
